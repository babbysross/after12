About Mind The Gap

Mind The Gap is a weekly podcast about new tech, gaming news and current events, with a healthy dose of banter. Presented by Stephen Perrie-Ujah and Paul Miller, an episode of MTG can be anything from an interview with an up and coming personal trainer, to a lengthy discussion about how 5G will change our lives.
